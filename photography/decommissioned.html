Satsop Nuclear Power Plant

While driving through eastern Washington in a rented cargo van for the Connections Museum, I spotted two cooling towers from the state highway and decided to investigate further.

View of the approach from Keys Road.

The uncompleted western containment structure provides views of the evergreen forest hillside behind it.

Similar to the containment structures, the base of the western cooling tower was not fully completed, and the interior space is much more sparse than the eastern companion. In late May, the hillside was covered in blooming yellow grasses and bushes.
