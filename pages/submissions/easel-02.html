Hi, I'm Claire Violet, a trans film photographer.

This is my submission to Easel 02: Panoramic film photos taken in Seattle and Oakland.

These images are part of an ongoing series I'm working on, where I have been examining maritime industrial and logistics infrastructure, but only one of them has appeared anywhere publicly. They were shot on Kodak Ektar (in Seattle) and Kodak Portra 160 (in Oakland) using a Hasselblad X-Pan. The first two have been cropped slightly. These photos are about size and color; The intricate details of these machines, their enormous scale, the way they effortlessly move— these photos are deeply erotic to me. Thanks for considering them.

Download a .zip with all photos as .jpeg files. If you'd like the full resolution .tif files, please send me an email.
